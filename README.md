# Look Learning landing page

Bespoke, single-page pitch for Enrique Cintolesi (CEO) and Renzo Lüttges Cintolesi (CTO) of Look Learning, prepared for eMerge Americas 2026 (Booth SS35). Built from Look Learning's own brand tokens: primary blue #216daf, Roboto, rounded pills, generous spacing, with the eye-shaped "O" motif from their wordmark carried forward as a single hero element.

Intended URL path: /for/look-learning

## Files
- `index.html` — page markup
- `styles.css` — all styles (no JS)
- `logo.png` — Look Learning wordmark (PNG, from brand pack)

## Notes
- CTA points to Matt Creamer's Calendly at https://calendly.com/thecro-eduba/30min
- No pricing or revenue terms surfaced (per sprint disclosure rules)
- All URLs are real anchors with `target="_blank" rel="noopener"`
- Eduba branding limited to footer "Prepared by Eduba, eduba.io"
- No external fonts beyond Roboto (already used on looklearning.com)
- Works clean at 375px mobile
