The 60 / 30 / 10 read

Here is how we would read your stack. In our experience, 60 percent of any AI product is traditional code and database work (session state, user progress, dashboards, billing). 30 percent is rule-based logic (curriculum alignment, age gating, safety filters, quiz scoring). 10 percent is genuinely AI (the avatar conversation itself, and the part of progress analytics that actually needs pattern matching).

Most small teams we meet put LLM calls in places that a Postgres query would serve cheaper, faster, and with better guarantees. The money you save there is the money you need for the margin on the voice conversation, which is the only place the model actually earns its keep.
